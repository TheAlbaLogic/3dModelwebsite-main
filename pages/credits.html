Interwar

The Renault R35 was a French light tank used during World War II armed with a 37mm gun and machine gun.

The FCM 36 was a French light infantry tank designed in the 1930s. It was armed with a 37mm gun and had a crew of two. Few were produced and they saw limited action in World War II.

The Char D1 was a French medium tank developed in the 1920s and 1930s. It was armed with a 47mm gun and saw limited action in World War II.

The AMC 35 was a French medium tank developed in the 1930s. It was armed with a 47mm gun and saw limited action in World War II.

The Somua S35 was a French medium tank designed in the late 1930s. It was armed with a 47mm gun and was considered one of the best French tanks of World War II.

The Char 2C was a French super-heavy tank developed in World War I and produced in the 1920s. It was armed with multiple guns and saw limited action in World War II.
